Analysis Metrics

Average Confidence
? Average confidence level across all 4 AI models. Higher percentage means models are more certain about their prediction.

Models Agreement
? How many models voted the same way. 4/4 = Unanimous (most reliable), 3/4 = Majority (reliable), 2/4 = Split (less reliable).
--
Reliability
? Overall prediction reliability. "Very High" = All models agree strongly, "High" = Strong majority, "Medium" = Majority with lower confidence, "Low" = Split decision or low confidence.
